\section{Conclusion and Future Work}
\label{sec:conclusion}
Afar-prgenv enables early evaluation of AMD compiler drops on Cray/HPE systems
by preserving the PE workflow while integrating AFAR compilers and ROCm
toolkits. The overlay converts isolated drops into PE-compatible environments
that can be validated with reproducible repro tests, giving support teams a
repeatable path to certify monthly drops before broader application testing.

Future work includes expanding the validation suite to cover additional Fortran
and OpenMP test suites, integrating application-level regression tests, and
automating readiness reports for new drops.
We also plan to formalize MPI swap-after-load tests, track ROCm runtime
compatibility for accelerator libraries, and integrate the repro harness with
site CI so that new drops are evaluated immediately after delivery.
